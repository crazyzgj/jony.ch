    Architecture and Components#

    The Huawei SD-WAN architecture is divided into three layers:

    1. Orchestration and Management Layer (iMaster NCE-Campus)#

    The unified brain of the entire network, providing network configuration, orchestration, and automated O&M capabilities.

    2. Control Layer (Route Reflector - RR)#

    Responsible for reflecting and distributing VPN routes and policy information among various sites.

    3. Forwarding Layer (NetEngine AR)#

    CPE devices deployed at branch enterprises and headquarters, responsible for actual data packet forwarding.
