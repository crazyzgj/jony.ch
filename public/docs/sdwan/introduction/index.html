    Huawei SD-WAN Introduction#

    What is SD-WAN?#

    Software-Defined Wide Area Network (SD-WAN) is a service that applies SDN technology to WAN scenarios. Through the separation of control and forwarding, as well as centralized management technologies, it realizes the intelligence of the WAN.

    Core Advantages of Huawei SD-WAN#

    • Simplified Interconnection: Supports multiple link access and hybrid networking.
    • Ultimate Experience: Application-level intelligent routing, ensuring the experience of core services.
    • Intelligent O&M: iMaster NCE provides visual centralized operations and management.
